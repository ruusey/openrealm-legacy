Heavy Weapons (scales off str)

Swords: The perfect balance between range attack speed and damage the basis of comparison for all other weapons
Axes: Sacrifices range for raw damage but remains consistent in attack speed
Hammers: Very low Attack speed with decent range and increased base damage per attack


Light Weapons (scales off dex)

Daggers: lower dps than swords with lower base damage and higher attack speed
Bows: slower than daggers with decent base damage but long range attacks
Chakrams: thrown blades that can deal decent damage with higher range than melee attacks but less than bows


Magic Weapons (scales off wis)

Tome: slower attack speed in exchange for higher base damage and piercing attacks with range similar to bows
Staves: standard attack speed with decent base damage lacks piercing but has the highest range of all weapons
Wands: faster attack speed decent base damage, but lower range than tomes

Armors
	Heavy armor/Plate armor, lowers Speed in exchange for the highest Defense buffs
	Light armor/Leather armor, medium Defense bonuses while also increasing Dexterity
	Cloaks/Cloth armor, lowest Defense stats of all three armors but heavy focus on Mana and Wisdom increases

Gauntlets

	Highly variable in bonuses with usually some defense and some offensive stats, less significant than armors but still important for a character build

Boots

	Big emphasis on Speed bonuses or drawbacks in exchange for more offensive stats or more defense, every boot should alter Speed in some level


Rings
	Being the cherry on top of the cake these will mostly focus on HP/MP either increasing them at the costs of less offensive stats or decreasing them to make you a true glass cannon, they can be mostly divided into defensive rings or offensive rings
